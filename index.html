Manifesto

Freedom is the absolute foundation; it grants the space to think, choose, and take action. While profit is necessary, work cannot be driven by it alone; my efforts must serve humanity honestly, ruling out industries that exploit rather than uplift.
With clarity - I act with enthusiasm. I am bold, expressive, and alive. I crave beauty and simplicity, both - appreciating them in the world and seeking to create them in my life's work.
I am a Visionary Catalyst. I thrive in the "zero-to-one" phase of work - where ideas are generated, strategies are formed, and prototypes are built. I possess high emotional intelligence and intuition, which I use to guide people and navigate ambiguity.
I believe strongly in a bias towards action as the only way to make progress: do, observe, analyze, learn, improve, repeat.
Conversely, I have a distinct aversion to administrative maintenance, "busy work," pencil pushing, absurd rules, and politics.
I love the genesis of things and I am a thinker who needs to see ideas take physical or actionable form. I engage with people through deep insight and genuine connection. I have a natural, automatic drive to understand the "why" and "how" behind problems, making me comfortable where others are anxious - in the unknown.
When the purpose aligns with my values, I become more than just a powerful advocate; I become the force that brings ideas to life and makes them happen.
